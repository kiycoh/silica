# User Refactoring Standards 
When refactoring notes in the `1 Cultura/` folder:
- **Atomicity**: Notes must be broken down into their smallest coherent concepts.
- **Wiklink-First**: Connectivity must be expressed in the *body* of the text using `[[Note Name]]`. Do not rely solely on YAML tags.
- **Completeness**: Ensure no semantic information is lost during decomposition.
- **Formatting**: Use Obsidian Flavored Markdown.
